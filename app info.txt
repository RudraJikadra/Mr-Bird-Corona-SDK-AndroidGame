How to Play:
>Your Screen is divided into two halfs vertically
>If you tap on the right side of your phone then the bird will get a force upwards and to the right, the same goes for the left side
>Then after tapping the right side, if you tap on the (opposite side)left side of your phone then the bird will get a force upward and to the left which will Nullify the right side force so that the bird will go up again


Caution: Don't tap twice on one side then the bird will get a double force on that side which will end the game

Score as many points as you can
Pass Mr Bird through as many pipes as you can
